Abstract

OPINO (OPINion ONtology) is an ontology for the formal representation of perspective-dependent opinions as Knowledge Graphs, grounded in the Description and Situation (DnS) Ontology Design Pattern and the DOLCE+DnS Ultralite foundational ontology. An opinion is modelled as a perspective-dependent description of an entity, premised on a construed state of affairs as perceived by a cognizer. OPINO introduces epistemic status gradation (Conjecture, Hypothesis, Assertion) and a provenance bridge to HiCO and PROV-O, and is designed to serve as a domain-agnostic interface alignable to CRMinf, Digital Hermeneutics, and nanopublication-based architectures.
